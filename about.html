About Eastgate Assembly Nyeri

Eastgate Assembly-KAG is a House of Worship, Prayer, and experiencing the presence of God. It is located in Nyeri, Kenya, Opposite of New bus Station and is part of the Kenya Assemblies of God (KAG) denomination.
Eastgate Assembly was founded in July 1996 by Bishop Dr. Wachira Karani and Pastor Margaret Wachira. The church faced many opposition from the surrounding people but God sustained it through prayer and faith. Our church has grown from a small gathering to a vibrant community of believers dedicated to worship, teaching, and outreach. We are committed to sharing the love of Christ with our community and beyond.


Our mission is to Worship God in Truth and Spirit and make disciples of all nations as per John 14:6, Acts 4:12, and John 3:16.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that transforms lives and impacts communities for the glory of God.

Our Mission

To reach the lost, disciple believers, raise leaders, and impact society through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Core Values

  • Biblical Truth – Grounded in the Word of God
  • Prayer & Worship – Joyful, reverent, and Spirit-led
  • Authentic Relationships – Genuine love and care for one another
  • Excellence – Honoring God through our best efforts
  • Servanthood – Equipping believers to lead and serve
  • Innovation – Embracing creativity in ministry
