CONNECT GROUPS

“Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap.” – Luke 6:38

Connect in Residential Homes

Connect Groups are small fellowship gatherings that take place in the homes of believers. They provide a space for prayer, Bible study, encouragement, and deeper relationships in the body of Christ. Through these home-based meetings, believers grow spiritually and build strong community bonds beyond the Sunday service.
