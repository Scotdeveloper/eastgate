Welcome to The EastGate Assembly Church Nyeri

“For I have chosen this Temple and sanctified it to be my Home forever; My eyes and Heart shall always be here” – 2 Chronicles 7:16

A place where Everybody is Somebody

Eastgate Assembly is a house of worship and prayer, a place to encounter the presence of God and find hope through sound biblical teaching delivered by Bishop Dr. Wachira Karani together with Pastor Margaret Wachira. We are passionate about worship, spiritual growth, and compassionate outreach. Whether you are new to faith in Jesus Christ, seeking deeper spiritual growth, or looking for a church family where you can worship, pray, and be nurtured, Eastgate Assembly is a place to belong—a home where you are welcome.

At Eastgate Assembly, we are committed to sharing the love of Christ with our community and beyond. Whether you are new in the faith or have been walking with Christ for many years, there is a place for you here. We invite you to join us in worship, fellowship, and service as we grow together in faith and love. Come and experience genuine fellowship and the life-changing love of Christ. If you are searching for a place to belong, we welcome you with open arms—because at Eastgate Assembly, EVERYBODY IS SOMEBODY!

About Us

Eastgate Assembly-KAG is a House of Worship, Prayer, and experiencing the presence of God. It is located in Nyeri, Kenya, Next to New bus Station and is part of the Kenya Assemblies of God (KAG) denomination.
