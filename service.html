Our Services

At Eastgate Assembly, we offer a variety of services designed to nurture your spiritual growth and strengthen our community. Join us for uplifting worship, engaging teaching, and meaningful fellowship.

Sunday Services

Main Service

Join us every Sunday from 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM for our English Service, followed by the Maranatha Service from 9:30 AM – 10:30 AM.
These are the first services of the day. Our second service runs from 10:30 AM – 1:00 PM, conducted in both English and Kiswahili.
Each service begins with 30 minutes of prayer, leading into a time of praise, worship, and the sermon.

Young Adults Service

Our Young Adults gather every Sunday from 8:30 AM – 10:30 AM for a vibrant service led by our dedicated leaders.
This is a time to worship, learn, and grow in faith together while also engaging in meaningful conversations on challenges affecting today’s youth and practical ways to overcome them.

Teenagers' Service

Every Sunday from 8:30 AM – 10:30 AM, our Teenagers Service provides a space for young people to connect with God and one another.
Led by our leaders, teenagers come together to worship, learn, and strengthen their faith, while also discussing the issues they face and finding biblical solutions to navigate them.

Children's Service

Parents, we invite you to entrust your children to our Children’s Ministry every Sunday from 8:30 AM – 10:30 AM. Under the guidance of caring and committed teachers, the children are nurtured in God’s Word through engaging lessons, worship, and fun activities.
Give your child the opportunity to experience God’s presence from a young age and grow in faith within a safe and loving environment.

Mid-week Services

Prayer Task Force

Tuesday is set apart as a day of prayer to pause mid-week and seek God’s guidance. It offers believers a moment to realign spiritually and intercede for families, the church, and the nation. This rhythm reminds us that breakthroughs come through time in God’s presence, not effort alone

Every Tuesday 5pm -6:30pm

Praise and Worship Rehearsal

A special time set aside for the choir to worship, study the Word, and prepare spiritually and musically as they serve in leading the congregation.

Saturday from 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM.

Women's Fellowship

A special gathering for women to connect, share the Word, pray together, and encourage one another in their walk with God.

Every Saturday 2pm - 4pm

Youth Fellowship

A dynamic time for our youth to gather, worship, and learn together, fostering spiritual growth and community among young believers.

Every Thursday 5pm - 6:30pm

First Saturday of Every Month

A special day to dedicate the new month to God through prayer, and to seek His blessings and guidance for the days ahead. For Godly direction and favor in all our endeavors.

Every Saturday 9am - 2pm
